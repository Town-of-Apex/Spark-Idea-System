What exactly is a Spark? ↓

A Spark is a tiny seed for a big change. It's not a fully-fledged project proposal yet — it's that "What if we..." moment that hits you during your morning coffee or while walking down Salem Street.

Examples of Great Sparks:

  • "What if we added solar benches to the Town Hall courtyard?"
  • "What if we digitized the temporary sign permit process?"
  • "What if we created a 'Welcome to Apex' kit for new business owners?"

The Upvote System:

Each person can upvote a Spark once. Upvotes help us understand collective interest and priority. You can always change your mind by clicking the vote button again.

Disclaimer: Sharing an idea doesn't guarantee implementation. Everything shared here is reviewed and considered seriously by the IT Innovations team, but constraints like budget, policy, and staffing apply. We're here to listen, explore, and ignite change together.
